Burrito

A classic Mexican burrito filled with seasoned beef, beans, rice, and fresh toppings wrapped in a warm tortilla.

Serving Size: 2 people

Preparation Time: 15 minutes | Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 200g ground beef
  • ½ cup cooked rice
  • ½ cup black beans, drained
  • ½ cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • ¼ cup chopped tomatoes
  • ¼ cup chopped lettuce
  • ¼ cup sour cream
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper

Equipment

  • Frying pan
  • Spatula
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Add ground beef, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook until browned.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a dry pan or microwave.
  4. Spread cooked rice and beans evenly on the tortillas.
  5. Add the cooked beef, cheese, tomatoes, and lettuce.
  6. Drizzle with sour cream and fold the burrito.
  7. Serve warm and enjoy!

Homemade Burger

A juicy homemade burger made with fresh ground beef, seasoned to perfection, and served with classic toppings.

Serving Size: 2 people

Preparation Time: 10 minutes | Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 300g ground beef (80/20 lean-to-fat ratio)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 hamburger buns
  • 2 slices cheddar cheese
  • 2 lettuce leaves
  • 2 tomato slices
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • 1 tbsp butter (for toasting buns)

Equipment

  • Mixing bowl
  • Frying pan or grill
  • Spatula
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine ground beef, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and Worcestershire sauce. Mix well.
  2. Divide the mixture into two equal portions and shape them into patties.
  3. Heat olive oil in a frying pan or grill over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the burger patties for about 3-4 minutes per side or until desired doneness.
  5. In the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheddar cheese on top of each patty to melt.
  6. Toast the hamburger buns with butter in a separate pan.
  7. Assemble the burger by spreading mayonnaise, ketchup, and mustard on the buns.
  8. Place the cooked patty on the bottom bun, add lettuce and tomato slices, then top with the other bun.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade burger!

Coxinha de Frango (Brazilian Chicken Croquette)

A popular Brazilian snack made with shredded chicken filling, wrapped in a creamy dough, breaded, and fried to golden perfection.

Serving Size: 4 people

Preparation Time: 40 minutes | Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp cream cheese
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Equipment

  • Frying pan
  • Pot
  • Wooden spoon
  • Knife
  • Cutting board
  • Deep fryer or large skillet

Instructions

  1. In a frying pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, sauté until golden.
  2. Add the shredded chicken and season with salt and pepper. Stir well.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth and cook until the liquid reduces. Add cream cheese, mix until creamy, then set aside to cool.
  4. In a pot, combine milk, butter, and a pinch of salt. Bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and add the flour all at once. Stir vigorously with a wooden spoon until the dough forms a ball and pulls away from the pot.
  6. Transfer the dough to a bowl and let it cool slightly.
  7. Take a small portion of dough, flatten it in your hand, and place a spoonful of chicken filling in the center. Shape into a teardrop form, sealing the edges.
  8. Dip each coxinha in beaten eggs, then roll in breadcrumbs.
  9. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or skillet to 350°F (175°C). Fry the coxinhas until golden brown, about 4-5 minutes.
  10. Drain on paper towels and serve warm. Enjoy!
